Step-By-Step Layering Instructions

  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Repeat Previous Steps

Baking Instructions

  1. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Cover with foil and cook for 25 minutes
  3. Remove the foil and cook for another 25 minutes
  4. Let sit for 15 minutes before cutting to allow juicyness
