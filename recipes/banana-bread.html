My Baby's delicious Banana Bread

In the Section we will be discussing how to create my baby's delicous banana bread. When biting into ths bread you will feel a surge of euphoria jolting through your body as if you were enveloped by and angel. enjoy

List of ingrediants you will need

  • 2 Cups of Flour
  • 1/2 Dark Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
  • 3 Whole ass ripe bananas
  • 2 MF eggs
  • 1 Tsp of vanilla
  • 1/2 Veggie oil

Instructions on how to make the delicious bread

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine dry ingrediants
  3. Combine butter and sugar in bowl, then add eggs & vanilla and mix well
  4. Add dry ingrediants and the previous mix together
  5. Pour into prepared loaf pan
  6. Cook for 60 minutes
