The Famous Never Been Feta Burger

In this section we will be going over how to make the delicous Never Been Feta Burger from Bob's Burgers

List of Ingrediants

  • QP of Crumbled Feta
  • 1 Lb of ground beef
  • 1/2 Cup Yellow Mustard
  • 1/4 Cup Mayonaise
  • 4 Small Ciabatta Rolls up in dis bih
  • Arugula
  • Sriracha (optional)

Detailed List on how to prepare the sandwhich

  1. Mix 3/4 cup feta with the beef and form 4 patties
  2. Cook them to your preference
  3. Mix the mustard and the mayonaise together in a small bowl
  4. Spread this on the tops of the rolls
  5. Put arugula on bottom part of roll (don't be scared)
  6. Then the burger
  7. Then more feta
  8. Then the Sriracha
  9. Eat the delicousness
